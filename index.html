BIOGRAPHY::

Dr. Dipawita Thakur is working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering, Modeling, Electronics and Systems (DIMES) at University of Calabria since 2023.

Prior to this, she was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Banasthali University, Rajasthan. She also worked as Technical Support Engineer in TechMahindra, India, and Project Tranee in Centre for Development of Advance Computing (C-DAC), India.

She received her Ph.D. from West Bengal University of Technology, Kolkata. Her primary research interests are in the domain of Smart healthcare using Machine Learning, specifically Performance Evaluation of and Human Activity Recognition Systems. She is the author of many scholarly research papers, including reputed journal papers, in the above mentioned areas.
